About LICK

A New York lifestyle brand, Long Island City Kleaners at its heart is an Art/Design studio. Rooted in many influences; including but not limited to: skating, painting, biking, film, and just about anything creative. We have appropriated the visage of a New York dry cleaners. A known place in your neighborhood to launder and freshen up your clothes. But also, in New York, a spot where everything might not always be what it seems.
